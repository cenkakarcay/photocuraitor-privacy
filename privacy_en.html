Privacy Policy

Effective date: 19 April 2026

Your photos stay on your device.

PhotoCuraitor is designed to run entirely on your iPhone. We do not upload, copy, view, or store your photos on any server. This policy explains what the app does and does not do.

Summary

  • The app processes photos locally using on-device AI.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.
  • No accounts, no sign-in, no tracking.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Information we access

To do its job, PhotoCuraitor requests access to your Photo Library. All scoring, grouping, and enhancement happens on your device. Photos never leave your iPhone and are not sent to us or to any third party.

Permissions

  • Photo Library - Required. Used to read, score, group, and let you delete photos locally.

Third parties

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or data brokers. The app does not contain tracking SDKs.

Children

PhotoCuraitor does not knowingly collect information from children. Because the app collects no data at all, it is suitable for users of any age permitted by the App Store.

Your rights

Since we do not receive or store any personal data, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete on our side. You retain full control of your photos through iOS.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the new version at the same URL.
